Hi, I'm Andrei Croitor, Creative Director at OQVA.digital.

I've always been fascinated by the way identity shapes our world. Over the years, I've developed two fundamental ideas that have become the core of my branding philosophy:

  1. "The sole purpose of any organisation is to provide the individual tools to build their own identity."

  2. "Consciousness is nothing but recognition of contrast."

I believe that by understanding and leveraging these principles, you can create a powerful, influential, and successful brand.

My philosophy is rooted in the concept that identity tools are the cornerstone of successful branding. By providing individuals with the tools to build their identity, you empower them to become true brand advocates. Consciousness is formed through recognising contrast, which allows us to create strong and impactful identities that resonate with our target audience.

I've developed an Identity Framework to help you build clear customer avatars and give your brand a competitive edge. This framework will guide you through the process of identifying the key individuals, events, locations, and sacrifices that shape your customers' identities.

By understanding and leveraging these elements, you can create a powerful connection with your audience and drive your brand's success.
